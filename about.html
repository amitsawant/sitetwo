About Me

Being a graphic designer, observation is an instinct for me. When the world sees the sky and the stars, I see the sparkle on each star, the slight twinkle with each move in its own universal space. Maybe this is what led me to my calling, creation.

I do not see myself as just a graphic designer, but as a humble creator. I wonder how the ultimate creator designed us so aesthetically well, but yet so functional. How nature formed mountains and rivers with such perfection, that anybody who sees it is not just amazed by it, but inspired. My wish is simple, to be able to achieve that with my work, to not just make viewers amazed, but to inspire them.

A happy go lucky guy, enthusiastic about work, fun-loving and a good kid to work with is what my colleagues would tell you about me, but for me its just a way of life. Live well, have fun, do good, create good work that outlives me. And that’s me.
